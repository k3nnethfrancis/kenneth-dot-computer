# Cybernetics

Foundational concepts in systems thinking, feedback, and the science of control and communication.

## Thinkers

- [W. Ross Ashby](/notes/cybernetics/thinkers/ashby) — Requisite variety, homeostasis
- [Gregory Bateson](/notes/cybernetics/thinkers/bateson) — Double bind, ecology of mind
- [Stafford Beer](/notes/cybernetics/thinkers/beer) — Viable System Model, management cybernetics
- [Nick Land](/notes/cybernetics/thinkers/land) — Accelerationism, hyperstition
- [Claude Shannon](/notes/cybernetics/thinkers/shannon) — Information theory
- [Francisco Varela](/notes/cybernetics/thinkers/varela) — Autopoiesis, enactivism
- [Norbert Wiener](/notes/cybernetics/thinkers/wiener) — Founder of cybernetics

## Concepts

- [Functionalism](/notes/cybernetics/functionalism) — Philosophy of mind: function over substrate

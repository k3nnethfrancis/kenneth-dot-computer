# Noometic AI

![Noometic AI](/images/quests/noometic-ai.jpg)

A creator-search product designed to make it easy for anyone to find Instagram & TikTok creators with natural-language queries. Building it taught us sometimes simple is better. Users wanted fast search and composable filters over precision vector searches. We added a BM25 fallback to help solve this (see [*"BM25 is all you need."*](/blog/bm25-is-all-you-need))

- [The vector search engine](https://github.com/NoometicAI/vector-creator-search)
- [BM25 creator search](https://github.com/k3nnethfrancis/bm25s_creator_search)
